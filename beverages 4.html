MILO DINASOUR

BACKGROUND

A Milo dinosaur is a chocolate malt originated in Australia. This kind of Milo variation recipe most commonly found in hawker centres, composed of a cup of iced Milo (a chocolate malt beverage) with undissolved Milo powder added on top of it. It is usually served cold to prevent the powder from immediately dissolving in the drink.

INDREGIENTS

  • 5tbsp. Milo Powder
  • 3/4 tbsp. condensed milk
  • hot water
  • 2 tbsp. extra Milo Powder as a topping
  • crushed ice

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix Milo Powder and Condensed milk in a tall glass.
  2. Add hot water halfway and stir well.
  3. Fill the glass with crushed ice then top with extra Milo Powder.

TIPS

  • The hot water dissolves the Milo faster but it is optional if you wish to skip it.
